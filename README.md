# [POWER BI] Sales Strategy Dashboard

Strategic Business Intelligence dashboard for global market expansion, product profitability, and risk analysis using Power BI.

---

# I. Introduction

## 1. Introduction to Dataset

This project analyzes global sales transactions to evaluate:
- market profitability
- product portfolio performance
- business growth trends
- operational risk
- customer acquisition behavior

The dashboard was designed to support senior managers in making strategic decisions related to:
- market expansion
- product prioritization
- profitability optimization
- operational risk management

The dataset includes:
- sales transactions
- regional management information
- returned order records

## 4. Business Questions

The dashboard was designed to answer the following business questions:

- Which markets generate the highest sustainable profitability?
- Which product categories contribute most to business growth?
- Which regions should be prioritized for expansion?
- Which categories and markets contain the highest operational risk?
- How does profitability evolve over time?
- How do customer acquisition and returning customer behaviors differ across markets?
